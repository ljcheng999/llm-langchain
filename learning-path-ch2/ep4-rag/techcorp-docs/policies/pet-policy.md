# TechCorp Pet Policy

## Pets in the Office

TechCorp welcomes well-behaved pets in our office on Fridays, designated as "Furry Fridays". Dogs and cats are allowed, provided they are up-to-date on vaccinations and have no history of aggression. Pet owners must register their pets with HR and provide vaccination records before bringing them to the office.

## Pet Owner Responsibilities

Pet owners are fully responsible for their pets' behavior and must clean up after them immediately. Pets must be kept on a leash or in a carrier when moving through common areas. If a pet becomes disruptive, the owner must remove them from the office immediately. We provide pet waste stations on each floor for your convenience.
