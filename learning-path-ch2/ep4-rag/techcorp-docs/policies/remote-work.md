# TechCorp Remote Work Policy

## Work From Home Guidelines

TechCorp employees are permitted to work remotely up to 3 days per week. This flexible arrangement helps maintain work-life balance while ensuring team collaboration. All remote work arrangements must be approved by your direct manager and documented with HR.

## Core Hours and Availability

When working remotely, employees must be available during core hours from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local timezone. During these hours, you should be reachable via Slack, email, and ready to join video calls. Outside core hours, you have flexibility to manage your schedule as long as you meet your deliverables.

## Equipment and Security

The company provides a laptop and necessary software licenses for remote work. Employees are responsible for maintaining a secure home network with password-protected WiFi. All work must be conducted on company-provided equipment, and use of VPN is mandatory when accessing company resources.
