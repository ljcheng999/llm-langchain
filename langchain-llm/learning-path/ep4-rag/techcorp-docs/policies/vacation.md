# TechCorp Vacation Policy

## Annual Leave Entitlement

All full-time employees receive 20 days of paid vacation per year, accruing at 1.67 days per month. New employees can start using vacation time after their first 90 days of employment. Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 10 days.

## Requesting Time Off

Vacation requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance for periods of 5 days or more. Shorter vacation periods require one week's notice. Your manager will review and approve requests based on team coverage and project deadlines.

## Holiday Schedule

In addition to vacation days, TechCorp observes 12 company holidays including New Year's Day, Independence Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas. The complete holiday calendar is published annually in January. Remote employees observe holidays based on their local country's calendar.
