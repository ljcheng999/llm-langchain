# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

**Q: What products does TechCorp offer?**
A: TechCorp offers CloudPro Enterprise for cloud storage and DataSync Professional for data synchronization. Both products are designed for enterprise customers with advanced security and compliance requirements.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Visit reset.techcorp.com and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.

## Billing and Pricing

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: TechCorp accepts credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for annual contracts. Purchase orders are accepted for amounts over $10,000. All prices are in USD unless otherwise specified.

**Q: Can I change my subscription plan?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade your plan anytime and downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Plan changes can be made through the billing portal at billing.techcorp.com. Refunds are not provided for mid-cycle downgrades.

## Technical Support

**Q: What are support hours?**
A: Standard support is available Monday-Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. Premium support customers have 24/7 access including weekends and holidays. Emergency hotline is always available for critical issues.

**Q: How quickly are support tickets resolved?**
A: Critical issues are addressed within 1 hour for premium customers and 4 hours for standard customers. Non-critical issues typically resolved within 1-2 business days. You can track ticket status at support.techcorp.com.
